## 8. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: Do I need a visa?**
A: All foreign nationals travelling to Kenya must apply for an Electronic Travel Authorisation (eTA) online prior to travel (formerly eVisa), costing approx USD 30-50. For Dubai and Thailand packages departing Kenya, we assist with visa processing.

**Q: What should I pack for a safari?**
A: Neutral-coloured clothing, light layers for cool mornings, walking shoes, sun hat, sunscreen, insect repellent, and binoculars.

**Q: Is travel insurance included?**
A: It is standard for International packages. For domestic bookings, please verify on your specific itinerary, but we strongly recommend independent cover.
